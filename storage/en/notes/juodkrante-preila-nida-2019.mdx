# Juodkrante, Nida, Preila, March 2019

All three points are on the Lithuanian side of the Curonian Spit. (I also have a [post](/blog/curonian-spit-2018/) about the Russian side.)

Juodkrante looks tidy.

![Street in Juodkrante on the bay shore](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/1.jpg)

The gulf coast is beautiful.

![View of the gulf coast](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/2.jpg)
![Old boats](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/3.jpg)
![Two swans](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/4.jpg)

Nice:

![View of the forest](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/5.jpg)

On the Hill of Witches, the wind in the crowns makes a noise very similar to the sound of the sea.

![Wooden sculpture at the entrance to the Hill of Witches](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/6.jpg)
![A path framed by trees](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/7.jpg)

Occasionally there is a sculpture-creepiness.

![Sculpture of a witch with a broom](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/8.jpg)
![Sculpture of a lost (?) woman with a lantern](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/9.jpg)

Nice:

![View of the pine forest again](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/10.jpg)

There are black houses in Nida! (I love black houses!) Nothing more to tell about it, off-season there is nothing happening there at all <nobr>:-)</nobr>.

![Black House in Nida](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/11.jpg)

If staying overnight is better, it's in Preila. You can rent a house right on the bay there.

![House by the bay](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/12.jpg)
![View of the bay](/images/content/juodkrante-preila-nida-2019/13.jpg)

It's kinda meditative.
